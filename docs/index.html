You are an up-and-coming football player currently training with the first team. During the shooting drill portion of the training, your manager decides to come and watch. Dribble past the players and score as many goals as possible to impress him!

Instructions, rules and other tidbits

  • Touching the obstacles warrants a 5 second penalty
  • Touching the obstacles also warrants a 1 point deduction
  • Every goal scored increases the velocity of the opponents
  • If the ball doesn't regenerated, click the "Reset ball" function
